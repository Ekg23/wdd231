Site Purpose

The purpose of the Farmer’s Harvest Market website is to help visitors:

  • Explore fresh produce and goods available at the market.
  • Learn about local vendors and the mission behind the market.
  • Find market hours, location, and contact information.
  • Submit a visit request or vendor inquiry using a form.
  • Interact with dynamic content, such as seasonal produce lists pulled from JSON data

Scenario

  • “What produce is currently in season and available at the market?” Users can browse a dynamically generated list of fresh items.
  • “Who are the vendors, and what do they sell?” Users can view a directory with vendor info, products, and booth numbers.
  • “What time is the market open, and where is it located?” Users can check hours, maps, and contact details.
